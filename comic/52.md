My good friend [Groovy drew me some Weskers for my birthday](https://blorbo.social/@ocelotmilk/116474140905739153), and I got inspired by her wonderful art to ponder Wesker and Zeno headcanon origins as little babbies in white dresses.  

I just want both these mean old stinky men to be traumatized little girls used/abused/dehumanized as test subjects much like Lisa or Sherry or Marie or... like every single other tiny babby gorl in the series😀 

And then I got to thinking about Zeno being younger than Wesker, but probably knowing about Wesker and growing up in the shadow of Project W.  But he'd choose to emulate Wesker as one of the few successes of project W, as every girl of his own "Project W", the memory transfer project, was a failure.  Why not become a success?  Thus ensues his yume-selfcest obsessive fanboy arc.  Totally not inspired by any interactions I've ever had with some interesting fans!  Totally not inspired by myself getting 😼 when Shane gets me a jacket that looks like Brock Samson's jacket...

He's one of the Emily/Spencer clones, and an asset of the stupid Connections probably from the ARK project being seized by them :P  And Gideon calls him a cheap imitation cuz, well, Gideon sees Z as a fanboy in cosplay.  Hell, Gideon is probably old enough to have perhaps watched over Project W if Gideon was old enough to be Spencer's protege.

I'm ready for all of this to be bullshit if they release a money grab DLC to try to patch up the many holes in the story of RE9.
